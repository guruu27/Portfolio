Twitter has been increasingly used by organizations to address customer reviews, complaints and feedback. Many users resort to using social media for immediate response to their queries.

But due to exceedingly large amount of data it becomes difficult for organizations to respond to every customer query or complaint. Some of these complaints might be urgent queries demanding immediate attention which due to vast data would be responded late.
